The Smurfs 2 : The Prisoner of the Green Stone

Developer : OSome-Studio
Publisher : Microids
Engine : Unreal Engine 4
Release date : 2023-11-02
Platforms : PC, Switch, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox Series

The Smurfs 2 : The Prisoner of the Green Stone is a dynamic platformer, action-adventure game in a third-person perspective with shooting mechanics.

Handy Smurf has created a revolutionary invention: SmurfoMix! However, it's missing a key ingredient, the Green Stone, which is in the greedy clutches of Gargamel. So Handy Smurf and his team set off on a mission to retrieve the famous stone from Gargamel's laboratory, but a fatal misstep causes it to explode, its fragments scattering throughout the Cursed Land.
In addition to unleashing into the wild its incredible powers to copy and decompose matter, the Green Stone has set free the evil Stolas, a new order bent on creating a reign of terror.
A crack team of 4 Smurfs must then adventure through various teleporters to find the fragments of the Green Stone and piece them back together before it plunges the world into chaos. Well equipped, the Smurfs can count on the SmurfoMix and an unexpected ally... Gargamel!

Asterix & Obelix XXXL : The Ram From Hibernia

Developer : OSome-Studio
Publisher : Microids
Engine : Unreal Engine 4
Release date : 2022-10-13
Platforms : PC, Switch, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox Series

Asterix & Obelix XXXL : The Ram From Hibernia is a platformer, action-adventure game in a top-down perspective.

In Hibernia, nothing goes to waste, but we throw it all away… Especially Romans.
The year is 50 B.C. Gaul is entirely occupied by the Romans. Well, not entirely. One small village of indomitable Gauls still holds out against the invaders…
Meanwhile, in Hibernia (today known as Ireland) Keratine, cheif Irishcoffix’s daughter, is calling Asterix and Obelix to the rescue as they need help to overcome the Roman invasion and bring back her father’s beloved golden ram. For this mission,you’ll control your favorite heroes and use your fists and any surrounding objects to make your way while throwing Romans in the air!
To progress in this pure action-adventure game, travel around the 6 new worlds and use objects around you to unlock access to hidden paths and, as usual, smash Romans that cross your way.
6 new worlds to explore from Gaul to Hibernia (Ireland) in an original story respectfully inspired by the iconic universe of our Gaulish heroes!
For the first time ever, play Asterix and Obelix in local cooperation up to 4 players on the same screen.
We offer you a new game experience by using a wide range of objects founds during your journey, but of course, you can still grab Romans !
New stylish combos and finish moves are available to beat them all!
This game has a high replay value due to challenges in each levels demanding skills to get success and rewards .
Series fans and casual gamers will also be delighted to get back to Asterix and Obelix world through this 3D game (top-down view) with stunning graphics and universal humour.

The Last Frame

Developers : Dorian ANTOINET, Laura BOUZEREAU, Victor DELENCLOS, Elie ENSUQUE, Raphaëlle GARCIA, Clémence PETIT, Clément RAMAT, Baptiste REY
In : 10 weeks for Ubisoft Game Lab Competition 2020
Engine : Unity
Platform : PC

The Ubisoft Game Lab Competition is an annual event organized by Ubisoft. It challenges students in groups of 8 to create a game in 10 weeks, with several constraints to fulfil and the valuable input of two Ubisoft mentors. I took part in the competition as a member of team UQAC-2. Unfortunately, the competition was cancelled around the 8th or 9th week due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

This edition's constraints were:

  • Build a game around the theme "generation"
  • Your game must offer online and LAN services
  • Your game must be cooperative
  • There must be at least one AI element in your game
  • There should be at least one player progression element in your game
  • Your game will be played by the jury for around 10 minutes

Following the cancellation of the contest, we decided to present The Last Frame at the ESAC competition 2020 : we won third place !

This competition was a great opportunity to work with new people, especially since we had regular meetings with our Ubisoft mentors. Their insight was really useful and helped us many times.

DataBurst

Developers : Dorian ANTOINET, Elie ENSUQUE, Anatole MARTIN, Clément RAMAT
In : 48 hours for WonderJam 2020
Engine : Unreal Engine 4
Platform : PC

This gamejam was organized by UQAC and sponsored by Ubisoft Saguenay, Beenox and Larian Studios. I am very proud to say that we reached 1st place and won the Jam with this game!

The theme was "Frenzy". Three styles were also imposed on our team: "Arcade", "Management" and "Exploration". Since we had to use at least 2 of these styles, we decided to go with "Arcade" and "Exploration".
The game is effectively divided into two parts:

  1. Exploration: the player is in the dark. The objective is to use a scanning device, controlled by a joystick, in order to spot the level's obstacles. These obstacles can be walls or traps.
  2. Frenzy: the level is lit up, the obstacles become invisible and the traps look exactly the same as regular data cubes. The player must navigate through the level in order to destroy the data cubes as fast as possible. Acquiring a data cube gives points. Encountering a wall or dashing into a trap takes points away. More points are obtained if the player is fast!
The objective is to reach the highest score possible!

Orbs

Developers : Dorian ANTOINET, Victor DELENCLOS, Augustin LEO, Clément RAMAT
In : 24 hours for Extra Jam 3
Engine : Unity
Platforms : PC, Web

Orbs was our game idea for Extra Jam 3, which is a game jam organized by Extra Life. The theme was "15 items or less".
Orbs is a simple and colourful looking game with 10 playable levels. Each level has a common objective : to retrieve all the colored orbs with a minimized number of moves. The player can manipulate his own orb with the mouse by adjusting the angle as well as the shooting power. On its way, the orb may encounter gadgets that will make it accelerate, bounce, attracted or even teleport him somewhere else! With each orb absorbed, the player's orb will grow (up to a certain limit) and will be able to aim for larger orbs.
Orbs makes extensive use of Unity's particle system and physics.
